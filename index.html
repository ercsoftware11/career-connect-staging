About

Who are we

Fully qualified professionals in the Career Development industry, specialising in guiding High School students with decision making for future employment and pathways. We also assist students with school subject selection including ATAR, QCE and VET pathways, as well as provide expert advice on all tertiary applications.

Our qualifications include:

  • Master of Educational Studies (Career Development)
  • Career Development Association of Australia Professional Member
  • Career Industry Council of Australia Professional Member
  • Queensland Association of Student Advisers Professional Member
